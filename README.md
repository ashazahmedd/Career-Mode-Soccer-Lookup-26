# Career Mode Soccer Lookup 26

A FIFA career mode-style transfer market. Look up players in the transfer
market, sign them to your squad within a transfer budget, and compare
players head-to-head by price, overall rating, potential, age, and wages.

Built with Next.js, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS. All progress (budget, squad,
comparisons) is stored locally in your browser — player names and ratings are
fictional and used for demo purposes only.
